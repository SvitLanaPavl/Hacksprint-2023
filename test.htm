About us

Who we are

We are two dedicated Holberton students, passionately teamed up for this trimester's Hacksprint! With complementary skills and a shared enthusiasm for coding, we bring a blend of creativity and technical skills to the table. Our collaboration aims to combine our strengths and make our project innovative and excellent.

How we work

Our partnership is built on fairness and collaboration. We believe in fair distribution of tasks, ensuring we are actively engaged and contributing to the project's success. Regular communication, brainstorming sessions, and a commitment to leveraging each other's expertise define our collaborative approach.

Our goals

As aspiring full-stack web developers, our ambitions fuel our dedication to this project. We envision using our skills across front-end and back-end development, mastering various programming languages, frameworks, and technologies essential in the web development landscape. Through this project, we aim to solidify our understanding, refine our problem-solving abilities, and create a tangible showcase of our capabilities.
